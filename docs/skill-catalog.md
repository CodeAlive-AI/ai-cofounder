# Skill Catalog

All 48 skills organized by category with descriptions and trigger phrases.

## CEO Operating System (12 skills)

### ceo-weekly-review
**Weekly CEO review** — dashboard across 9 categories, 10 strategic questions, self-deception check, next week focus.
- Triggers: "weekly review", "how was the week"
- Frequency: Weekly (Sunday evening cron or manual)

### hypothesis-tracker
**Hypothesis management** — add, update, rank, and kill hypotheses with WIP limits (max 3 active), evidence tiers, ELV scoring, and relay race rules.
- Triggers: "new hypothesis", "update hypothesis", "show hypotheses"

### decision-playbook
**9 quick-reference decision frameworks** — when to double down, pivot, change ICP, change positioning, kill a feature, scale sales, hire, diagnose low conversion.
- Triggers: "should we...", "when to...", "decision about..."

### strategic-review
**Deep multi-phase strategic analysis** — premise challenge, alternatives (min 2), mode selection, dream state mapping, risk map, adversarial review.
- Triggers: "strategic review", "let's think strategically", "analyze the decision"

### business-investigation
**Root cause analysis** — systematic debugging of business problems. Iron law: don't propose solutions without investigating root cause. 3-strike rule for hypothesis testing.
- Triggers: "why no signups", "why churn", "investigate", "dig into"

### discovery-debrief
**Post-conversation structured debrief** — extract learnings (who, demand reality, status quo, narrowest wedge, surprise), assess signal tier, update hypotheses, detect patterns.
- Triggers: "talked to a customer", "debrief", "had a call with"

### metrics-briefing
**CEO-level metrics interpretation** — 9 categories: market learning, product value, usage, pipeline, retention, B2C->B2B, execution, economics, PMF detection.
- Triggers: "metrics", "how are our numbers", "dashboard"

### anti-pattern-audit
**Monthly self-deception audit** — 25 anti-patterns in 5 blocks: general founder, ex-engineer, metric interpretation, pre-PMF growth, AI-era. Scored 0-2 per pattern.
- Triggers: "anti-pattern audit", "self-deception check", monthly cron

### idea-generator
**Generate business/marketing/content ideas** — with validation filters (Why Now test, tarpit detection, differentiation check) and red flags check.
- Triggers: "give me ideas", "brainstorm", "what should we try"

### daily-recap
**End-of-day consolidation** — summarize daily log, promote durable facts to MEMORY.md, identify open threads, check constraint alignment.
- Triggers: automatic (heartbeat at evening window), "recap the day"

### structured-log
**Structured daily logging** — write entries with TYPE tags (DECISION, FACT, INSIGHT, TOOL, TASK, HYPOTHESIS), facts, concepts (for search), and file references.
- Used automatically by other skills after meaningful work

### fpf-problem-solving
**First Principles Framework** — transdisciplinary reasoning architecture. Use for complex decomposition, high-stakes decisions, cross-domain analysis, systematic alternative generation.
- Triggers: "think through this rigorously", "FPF", "first principles"
